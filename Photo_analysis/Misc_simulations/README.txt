*** Overview of the miscellaneous analysis documents ***


The files in this folder provide a method for additional theoretical investigations to support the analysis.

- geometric ray analysis: basic tools to look into the results of geometric ray analysis
- prop angle: investigations into the propagation angle of hollow beams in different axicon set-ups.
- scale effects: an investigation into the effect of imaging and the use of the power law to characterise the trap profile.
